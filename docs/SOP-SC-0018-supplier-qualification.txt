1. PURPOSE
This SOP defines the process for qualifying, maintaining, and 
requalifying suppliers of GxP-relevant materials and services 
for PharmaCo manufacturing operations. It ensures all active 
suppliers meet regulatory and quality requirements before and 
during their engagement with PharmaCo sites.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all external suppliers providing raw materials, 
packaging components, contract manufacturing services, and 
laboratory services to any PharmaCo manufacturing site.

3. GXP CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
3.1 All suppliers of GxP-critical materials must hold a current 
GxP certification issued by a recognised regulatory authority.
3.2 GxP certificates must be renewed annually and submitted to 
the PharmaCo Supplier Portal at least 30 days before expiry.
3.3 Suppliers must notify PharmaCo immediately if certification 
is suspended, withdrawn, or materially changed.

4. INITIAL QUALIFICATION PROCESS
Step 1: Supplier submits qualification package including GxP 
certificate, quality manual, and site audit reports.
Step 2: Supply Chain Data Office reviews documentation and 
assigns qualification score.
Step 3: Quality team conducts remote or on-site audit for 
high-risk suppliers.
Step 4: Qualified suppliers are registered in the Supplier Master 
system (Reltio) and assigned Approved status.
Step 5: Collibra catalog updated with supplier CDE metadata 
including certification expiry date.

5. ANNUAL REQUALIFICATION
5.1 All GxP suppliers must be requalified annually.
5.2 Requalification is triggered automatically 90 days before 
GxP certificate expiry.
5.3 Supplier must submit updated certification documents 
within 30 days of receiving requalification notice.
5.4 Failure to submit within 30 days triggers Conditional status.
5.5 Failure to submit within 60 days triggers Suspended status.

6. CERTIFICATION LAPSE PROCEDURE
6.1 If a supplier's GxP certificate expires without renewal, 
the supplier is immediately moved to Suspended status.
6.2 All open Purchase Orders with the suspended supplier must 
be placed on Quality Hold within 24 hours.
6.3 No new Purchase Orders may be raised for a Suspended supplier.
6.4 The Procurement Lead must notify the site Operations Manager 
within 4 hours of suspension.
6.5 A CAPA must be initiated within 5 business days referencing 
the supplier qualification failure.
6.6 Reinstatement requires full requalification audit and 
Quality Director approval.

7. SUPPLIER STATUS DEFINITIONS
Approved: Current GxP cert, fully qualified, POs permitted.
Conditional: Cert expiring within 90 days, requalification in 
progress, existing POs permitted, no new POs above threshold.
Suspended: Cert lapsed or audit failed, all POs on Quality Hold.
Disqualified: Permanently removed, never to be reinstated.

8. ESCALATION MATRIX
Day 0 — Cert expires: Auto-suspend, Quality Hold on all open POs.
Day 1 — Procurement Lead notified.
Day 3 — Operations Manager notified.
Day 7 — Quality Director review meeting.
Day 30 — Disqualification review if no corrective action.
